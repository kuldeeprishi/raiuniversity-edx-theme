## About Section ##

About Rai University

Rai University is developing new online course material in order to improve on-campus courses and provide broader public access to exciting learning opportunities.

The engineering team at Rai University for Online Learning is running and developing on an instance of the OpenEdX platform. More details are on MOOCS Magazine.

## FAQ Section ##

FAQs

Has Rai University joined the edX consortium?
No. We're partnering on the open source software.

What browsers are recommended for use with Rai University OpenEdX?
The latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Apple Safari. (The site is currently not optimized for mobile use.)
